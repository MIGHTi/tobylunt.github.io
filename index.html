Our Vision

What if you could feed people and animals a high quality protein that requires less water, fewer resources, and minimal labor comparted to traditional livestock?
At MIGHTi, we believe insects are the solution.

Low Water Use

Farming insects uses approximately 1/10th the water of raising beef for protein.

Low Tech

MIGHTi's insect farming kits and solar dryers can be made with local materials.

Economic Engine

Insects can be farmed year-round, providing a key income source through "hunger seasons".

Nutritionally Rich

Insects are high in protein, as well as many essential amino acids and other nutrients.

 

CORE PRINCIPLES OF MIGHTi

Participatory

At MIGHTi, all partners are contributing to the mission and vision of the project, and drive programming collectively.

Sustainable

MIGHTi is committed to leveraging climate smart, innovative methods to improve environmental outcomes as well as boosting livelihoods.

Gender Sensitive

75% of agricultural labor in developing countries is performed by women. Working with women's coops helps empower them and their families.

About MIGHTi

MIGHTi is a collaborative research project designed to address the multifaceted and systematic factors that contribute to food insecurity in Southern Africa while simultaneously joining a global effort to re-envision food systems in a changing climate.
